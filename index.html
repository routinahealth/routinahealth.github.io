routina

your mental health companion.

routina is a mental health application focused on personalization.

have you or a loved one been diagnosed with depression? maybe anxiety? a sprinkle of ADHD? worry not! routina is a mental health application made for you.

we at routina labs incorporated (not actually incorporated) understand that sometimes it's hard to keep a routine, to keep a schedule, to not procrastinate. this project itself is a product of procrastination!

let's cut to the chase. routina is an application made for those who struggle with schedules, by those who struggle with schedules.

it features:


a simple, readable schedule

customization


bonuses!

professional help


it looks simple, and that's because it is! routina is built for simplicity, so you don't have to put as many brain units into your new schedule!

we know you're asking:
where can i download this?
well, it's not available for download yet.

we know, it's sad. in the future, though, routina will be released as a mobile app, and will later be released as a desktop app across all operating systems!

we have high hopes for routina; we want to make an application that everyone will be happy to use (no matter how old or young!)

we believe that making a schedule into something a bit more fun (like the bonus shop) will make people at least a little more excited to use their schedule.

we're as
thrilled
for the future as you are.
