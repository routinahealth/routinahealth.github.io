our goals

we have goals. a lot of them.

  • we want to try to alleviate mental health issues in teens. we know how hard it is sometimes, and our hopes are to at least make it a little easier on you.

  • provide friendly, helpful, and most importantly, human support.

  • create a (mostly) bug-free application that is easy to use and unintimidating.

  • build a development team with similar goals: helping people.

we know, promises aren't always perfect

but we assure you, we will do everything in our power in order to make sure that this product succeeds. at the least, a working, mostly feature-rich application will be available by the end of 2022.
