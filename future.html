the future

routina is starting from the ground up.

an application built off the hopes and dreams of a couple students, routina's plans are big.

things won't be perfect from the start: there will likely be developmental delays (especially with the pandemic), there will be bugs to squash, but in the end there will be a product.

when will routina be released?

more info can be found on our download page, but as of January 2022, the release goal is for the end of the year.

will routina continue to be free?

our intentions are to keep routina free to use for the entirety of its lifespan. whether we have to resort to in-app ads or donations, we want to make the app as accessible to the public as possible. we want to help, not hinder.
